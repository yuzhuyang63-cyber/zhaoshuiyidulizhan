# YouTube Video Upload Plan

After uploading each product video to YouTube, paste the video ID into `productYouTubeVideoIds` in `script.js`, and paste the upload date into `productYouTubeUploadDates` using `YYYY-MM-DD` format. The product detail page will then use the YouTube embed automatically and keep the local MP4 only as a fallback.

## Recommended YouTube Settings

- Visibility: Public or Unlisted before final review; Public when ready for SEO.
- Title: keep the product model and "groundwater detector" near the front.
- Description: include water detector, groundwater finder, Middle East use, drilling, agriculture, WhatsApp contact, and website URL.
- Chapters: add simple timestamps such as Product overview, Field setup, Operation flow, Application scenarios.
- Thumbnail: use a clear product or field application image, not a dark frame.
- Playlist: create a playlist named `Groundwater Detector Product Videos`.
